I am creating my first webpage in Tailwind CSS. It is very hard as we have to memorize the classes. Tailwind CSS can initially feel overwhelming for newcomers due to its unconventional approach to styling. Unlike traditional CSS frameworks that offer pre-designed components, Tailwind requires you to build your designs using utility classes. This can mean memorizing a myriad of class names and understanding how to combine them effectively. Additionally, the lack of predefined templates might be daunting for those accustomed to plug-and-play solutions. While Tailwind’s flexibility is its strength, it also means designers and developers have to invest time in mastering its methodology, which might slow down initial adoption."
